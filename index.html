How to make it at home

Before begin, you will need to make your bot recognise your face, and you will need this tutorial by Lofi robot. It's the foundation of the project, and it'll be very useful for the rest.

The materials you will need
  • 1 x BBC micro:bit v2
  • 2 x servo motors (SG90 type)
  • 1 x robot head or support (cardboard or 3D printed)
  • 1 x jumper wires
  • 1 x battery/power supply
  • 1 x micro:bit's cable
